About Medrath Pet Farm

Medrath Pet Farm is a peaceful sanctuary nestled in the heart of the countryside, dedicated to providing a safe and loving home for a diverse range of animals. The farm is home to more than 25 species, each cared for with compassion and expertise.

Whether you're visiting with family, school groups, or as an animal enthusiast, Medrath Pet Farm offers an immersive experience that brings people closer to nature and the animal kingdom. We believe in education through interaction, allowing visitors to learn about animal behavior, sustainable farming practices, and the importance of responsible pet care.
